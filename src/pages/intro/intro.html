What is the GIFT Platform?

The GIFT Platform is a set of tools, including this app, that lets you make and get 'gifts'.
It is a service provided by the EU-funded GIFT project, which is investigating ways to combine digital content with real cultural artifacts - museum exhibits, historical sites, and so on - to provide new ways for visitors to engage with heritage.

What is this app?

You may have already been sent a gift by another user. The gifts are personal messages created by other users for you to experience when you have found particular exhibits in a museum.
This app lets you receive your gifts, and helps you find the perfect situations to unwrap and experience them.
